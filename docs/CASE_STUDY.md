# Case Study: Intake

## Context
Sales teams often struggle with inbound leads arriving in various formats (unstructured emails, incomplete forms, messy partner referrals). Manually parsing this data into a CRM takes time and is prone to error.

## Solution
Intake provides a centralized pipeline that ingests raw lead submissions, uses AI to extract structured business fields, and presents them to a human reviewer for approval.

## Value Delivered
- **Efficiency**: Reduces time spent manually entering data.
- **Accuracy**: AI highlights missing information and provides confidence scores.
- **Accountability**: Full audit logs for every state change.
- **Quality Control**: Human-in-the-loop ensures only high-quality data reaches the CRM.
